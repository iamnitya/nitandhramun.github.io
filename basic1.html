Welcome to Model United Nations

Model United Nations (MUN) is a simulation of the United Nations where students play the roles of diplomats from different countries and attempt to solve real-world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned countries.

About Us

Our organization is committed to promoting global awareness, leadership, and diplomacy among young people through Model United Nations. We offer opportunities for students to participate in MUN conferences, where they can improve their public speaking, critical thinking, and research skills.

Model United Nations has a long history dating back to the 1920s. It started as a way to educate students about international relations and diplomacy. Today, MUN conferences are held around the world and attract thousands of students every year.

If you are interested in getting involved in Model UN, please contact us or check out our Conferences section for more information.
